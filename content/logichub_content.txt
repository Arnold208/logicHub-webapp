LOGICHUB — CORE DESCRIPTION (GLOBAL REFERENCE)

LogicHub is a makerspace, innovation hub, and product development environment based in Ghana.
We exist to help people turn ideas into real, working products.

At LogicHub, students, entrepreneurs, creatives, engineers, and innovators can walk in with an idea—no matter how early—and access the tools, guidance, and environment needed to bring it to life.

From concept to prototype, and from prototype to product, LogicHub provides the space, expertise, and community that make innovation practical and achievable.

Our work spans digital fabrication, hardware and product development, incubation, learning programs, and impact-driven innovation.

LogicHub is building the foundation for Ghana’s next generation of makers, startups, and technology-driven solutions.

What Is LogicHub

LogicHub is a collaborative space designed for people who want to build.
Whether you are a student with a project idea, an entrepreneur developing a startup, or a creative exploring new possibilities, LogicHub provides the tools and support to help you move forward.

We combine modern fabrication technologies, technical expertise, and a culture of learning to help innovators turn imagination into reality.

Our Mission

To empower innovators with the tools, knowledge, and environment needed to transform ideas into impactful products.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading makerspace and innovation hub—where young people and forward-thinking creators build solutions that shape the future.
